## Strategy

The Collective Vendor focuses on:

- **Modern chase cards** — High-demand cards from recent sets with strong liquidity
- **Grading arbitrage** — Buying raw cards with high grade potential, submitting to PSA/CGC, selling graded
- **Show sourcing** — Leveraging in-person connections at major Pokémon shows for below-market deals
- **Seasonal timing** — Aligning buys/sells with set releases, holidays, and market cycles
